# ByteBudget – AI for Bharat Hackathon

ByteBudget is an AI-powered spending and savings assistant designed to help users work smarter with money by turning daily expenses into clear insights, month-end predictions, and actionable nudges.

Built with a privacy-first, India-first approach, ByteBudget focuses on simplicity, learning, and productivity rather than cluttered charts.

---

## Problem Statement
Many users struggle to understand where their money goes and what actions to take next. Existing finance apps focus on tracking and charts, leaving users confused, overwhelmed, and inconsistent with saving habits.

---

## Solution Overview
ByteBudget solves the “money disappears” problem by:
- Automatically organizing expenses
- Predicting month-end spending and overspend risk
- Explaining spending behavior in plain language
- Nudging users toward better savings decisions

The app is designed for one-glance understanding with minimal UI and no financial jargon.

---

## Target Users
- Students
- First-time earners
- Young professionals
- Cash-heavy users in India

---

## Key AI Capabilities
- **Predictive Analysis (ML):** Forecasts month-end spend and overspend risk using historical pace, category variance, and recent spikes
- **Behavior Learning:** Learns personal spending patterns such as weekend bias and frequent small spends
- **LLM-based Assistant:** Converts analytics into simple explanations, answers queries, and provides personalized nudges

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React / Streamlit
- **Backend:** FastAPI (Python)
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas
- **Analytics & ML:** Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn
- **AI Assistant:** Amazon Bedrock (LLMs)
- **Cloud:** AWS Lambda, API Gateway, S3, CloudWatch

---

## Privacy & Bharat-first Design
- No bank linking required
- Supports manual & cash expense logging
- Minimal data storage
- Simple language explanations for first-time finance learners

---

## Success Metrics
- Faster money decisions (insights in seconds)
- Reduced overspending incidents over time
- Improved savings consistency across weeks
